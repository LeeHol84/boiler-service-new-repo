Birmingham Boiler Service has got you covered!

When considering a Boiler Service in Birmingham, we know that two of the most pressing issues on peoples minds are:

How do I get a Boiler Service Near Me that's both Great Value and performed by a Trustworthy Engineer?

And:

If I hire an Engineer to Service my Boiler will the work be performed to a high standard and will it be Covered by a Guarantee?

With Birmingham Boiler Service we you won't have to worry any more as our great value service is performed by certified engineers, whose work is guaranteed.

Birmingham Boiler Service is here to help with all your boiler servicing needs. Ideally a Boiler service should be performed once a year, however it is regularly overlooked or sometimes even completely forgotten. It is extremely important to make sure your boiler is working correctly and regular boiler servicing with Birmingham Boiler Service can help you avoid these two potential issues.

  • The first is that your boiler may be costing you extra money on your energy bills without you even realising it, due to the boilers inefficiency.

  • The second is that without regular services and maintenance an undiscovered and unresolved issue could be potentially dangerous.


    • A boiler service can also identify any problems with your boiler and address them before they do major, and potentially costly damage to your heating system.

    A boiler service costs considerably less than replacing your boiler completely.

    According to the department of health, 4000 people go to A & E each year with carbon monoxide poisoning as a result of a boiler not being safe. Having a boiler service can give you peace of mind that your boiler is running safely.

    Boiler services are also vital for making sure you maintain your manufacturers warranty, failure to get it serviced can void your warranty and affect your ability to make claims.
